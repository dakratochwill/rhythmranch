Rhythm Ranch Homestead Visit

It's kidding season at Rhythm Ranch Homestead, and everyone wants to hug a baby goat! We are currently booking 40-minute sessions, which include:

  • view or pet adult and baby goats, chickens, roosters, ducks, and bunny
  • hold a baby goat in an enclosed area
  • take a tour of the property, including the barn and chicken coop
